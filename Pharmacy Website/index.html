About Us

Preston Medical Pharmacy is an independently owned and operated pharmacy and home health care centre located in Cambridge, Ontario, Canada. We have certified professionals on staff at all times to assist you with any concerns you may have about your health care needs.

Our services

Compliance Packages

Blister packaging to improve medication administration & increase adherence to drug therapy.

After Hours Medication Service

Evenings, Sundays, holidays, for those emergencies – when you need help, Preston Medical Pharmacy has you covered.

INR Clinic

This is a pharmacist managed anticoagulation program utilizing point-of-care INR monitoring and a medical directive from the referring prescriber.

Diabetes Education

Preston Medical Pharmacy has 3 Certified Diabetes Educators available on staff for all patients with diabetes.

QUIT Smoking Program

This program includes 9 one-on-one discussions between the pharmacist and the patient with a 12 month follow-up period.

Specialty Compounding

Preparation of dosage forms not commercially available for special needs.

Medscheck

A free one-on-one, private, 20 to 30 minute consultation with one of our pharmacist to discuss how all current medications, prescriptions and over-the-counter, you are taking interact with each other. For further information please go to www.medscheck.ca.

Printed Medication Information Sheets

  • Printed facts about your new prescription

  • Personal medication wallet cards listing all your current medications

  • Magazines & pamphlets on health care and disease states
